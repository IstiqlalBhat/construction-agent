## Trade Selection by Project Value

- **>$50M**: All 10 divisions
- **$10M-$50M**: Div 3, 5, 9, 22, 23, 26, 31
- **$5M-$10M**: Div 3, 9, 22, 23, 26
- **<$5M**: Div 3, 23, 26
